Our Favorites

Moises's Current Favorite

My favorite food spot is the classic Alladin Gyro-Cery in UW! It's open until 3 am and they serve the best late night food. The prices are good for the quality and amount you get!

Adela's Current Favorite

My favorite food spot right now is Boiling Point right next to Uwajimaya! It has a bunch of options including vegan friendly, and it's a great place to eat for bigger groups. It's the best of both worlds with soup, meat, noodles, and veggies!

Henry's Current Favorite

My favorite food spot right now has to be Coche Valley! It's a dessert cafe with Korean style shaved ice called Bingsu. They have a bunch of different foods including snacks and drinks. My favorite order there is the Mango Cheesecake Bingsu

Ivanna's Current Favorite

My favorite place to eat right now is Daawat Grill in downtown! Although it's a little on the pricier end, the food is authentic and so worth your time!
