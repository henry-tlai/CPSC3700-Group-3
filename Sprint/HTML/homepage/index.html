Bite Into Seattle

Welcome to Bite Into Seattle! Your ultimate guide to the hidden gems and iconic places to eat that define Seattle's culinary landscape. Whether you're a local foodie looking for the latest trends or a visitor searching for authentic PNW palates, our reviews and recommendations will have you covered!

Koko's Restaurant and Tequila Bar

Tried the new Koko's Restaurant in Cap Hill and the vibe was excellent, the music was great, drinks were delicious, and the food was authentic!

Only stopped by for a couple of drinks and a side, but it's very homey and welcoming and a great place for a fancy night out or get together with friends.

I tried the chips and salsa, and it was fresh, homemade, and incredibly yummy!

Paiks Noodles

Amazing food, love hot pot! Fun experience for a bigger group of people to go together.

Worth the price and always come out super full!

Going to this place has become a family tradition everytime they visit!

Tavolàta

Handmade pasta and it's incredibly delicious!

Didn't make the happy hour, but they have great happy hour deals if you want to save some money!

In a great location and the vibe of the restaurant is very fun for a good night out.
