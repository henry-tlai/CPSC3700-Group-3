Koko's Restaurant and Tequila Bar

Adela Eccli's Review

May 10, 2024

Tried the new Koko's Restaurant in Cap Hill and the vibe was excellent, the music was great, drinks were delicious, and the food was authentic!

Only stopped by for a couple of drinks and a side, but it's very homey and welcoming and a great place for a fancy night out or get together with friends.

I tried the chips and salsa, and it was fresh, homemade, and incredibly yummy!
