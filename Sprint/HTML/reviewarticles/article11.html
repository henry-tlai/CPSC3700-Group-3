D' La Santa

@niharswami Review

May 20, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

D' La santa has a delicious dinner menu. They just started serving brunch on weekends. We decided to try it out and honestly was amazing, 100% recommended

They have pan dulce and mexican hot chocolate.If you are going for brunch try the gordita with chicharrón verde.

They have a tree in the middle of the dinning area, lovely!
