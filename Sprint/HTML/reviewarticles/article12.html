Pink Salt

@raj Review

May 3, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Perfect spot in Seattle to find delicious peruvian food! We tried the ceviche, lomo saltado and seafood rice. Everything was outstanding!

I don't find an easy task to get peruvian food in Seattle. This places is going to be my spot for whenever I am craving a good quality ceviche.

It is a little pricey, but it is worth.
