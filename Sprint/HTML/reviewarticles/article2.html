Migoto

@nosh228 Review

May 12, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Reoccuring guest at Migoto's! Love this place's sushi, drinks, and surprisingly their udon!

The servers and workers there have been there forever and are the sweetest! They always give a complimentary dessert after and it's right next to the Seattle University campus.

My favorite dish there is the avocado roll with shrimp tempura!
