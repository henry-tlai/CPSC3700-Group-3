Kobuta & Ookami Katsu and sake house

@iheartf00d Review

April 24, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Went here three times in one month! I always get the cheesy tonkatsu, and it always hits!

It's a pretty small restaurant and it fills up fast, but the wait isn't too long and it's totally worth it!

I tend to have leftovers since the portion size is pretty big and so you definitely get your money's worth.
