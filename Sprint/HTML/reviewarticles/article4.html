Koko's Restaurant and Tequila Bar

@jinsu_jinwoo Review

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

December 14, 2023

Worked here for about a year and always got complimentary food during shifts.

One thing I can say in confidence, is that I've never gotten sick of the food. It's quality and it's great!

It is definitely pricey and not a restaurant I would regularly go to if I paid for it myself, but when you want quality and a nice dinner out, this place is the place to indulge in!
