Rhein Haus Seattle

@swimchick306 Review

March 6, 2022

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Super yummy and definitely glad I went! Not my first choice of cuisine, but I did like it!

A good hot dog after a night out always hits, so I'd recommend and pricing is not too bad for what you get.

There's definitely better restaurants in Seattle, but if you're looking for this type of cuisine, I recommend!
